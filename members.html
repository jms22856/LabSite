Principal Investigator

ResearchGate Biosketch

Paul Lindahl

I was born and raised in Chongqing, China, and moved to the United States at age 15. I earned my bachelor’s degree in chemistry from Texas A&M University in 2022 and joined the Lindahl Lab in 2023. My research focuses on identifying and characterizing non-transferrin-bound iron (NTBI), a toxic form of iron implicated in hereditary hemochromatosis. Outside the lab, I enjoy traveling, shopping, watching anime, and spending time with my family.
